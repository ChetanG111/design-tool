ALLOWED EXPRESSIVE TECHNIQUES (FINITE SET)

HERO LAYOUTS:
Text-level emphasis:
• Background highlight behind text (never text color)
• Underlines, strike-lines, or side rules
• Very subtle rotation of emphasized text
• Offset positioning of emphasized text relative to baseline

Container-level emphasis:
• Framed or inset hero container (e.g. content bounded within ~90% of viewport)
• Subtle border or outline around hero region
• Intentional negative space framing (canvas effect)
